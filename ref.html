National Instruments STS

  • NI Semiconductor Testing System ⏏

The National Instruments Semiconductor Testing System (STS) houses a PXIe-1078 outfitted with the following cards:

Model Card Type Description
PXIe-4140 PXIe Card 4-CH Precision SMU
PXIe-6556 PXIe Card Digital Waveform Generator/Analyzer with PPMU
PXIe-2567 PXI Card 64 Pin Relay Driver
The STS is located on Bench 6
