How the Lab Works

  • The PSPE lab will operate weekdays from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
  • Students will reserve days and times to use the lab.
  • Lab access will be restricted.
  • Students are expected to complete all pre-requiesites before they enter the lab.
  • Unprepared and unqualified students will not be allowed in the lab. No exceptions.
  • Students will be responsible for knowing the rules of the lab provided to them.
  • Students will use this site to make reservations, click "Next" to learn the process.
