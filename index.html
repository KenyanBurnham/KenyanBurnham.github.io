Welcome, this page will give a brief overview of the innerworking of the lab, the features of the site, and recomendations on how to make the most of this student resource.



How the lab is operated.
The PSPE Lab lab is setup like an industrial testing facility.
  • The lab is open and accessible 24 hours/7 days a week.
  • Individuals can use the equipment that is made available.
  • Those that reserve a bench get priority.
  • Special equipment requires a request to have it placed in the lab during business hours.

Step 2: Plan Your Test





Research your device.

Generally, you need to build a device interface board and choose what equipment you need. The following questions may be helpful when planning your test:


  • What test am I being asked to perform?
  • What configuration modes or setting does the device have internally?
  • How long do I have to complete this test?
  • When will I have the time to complete this test?





Research Equipment.

All available equipment is listed on Equipment Page. When you reserve a bench you can add equipment in addition to what is already located in the lab.


Here is a list of questions you might consider when researching equipment options:

  • Will I need more than one channel?
  • What resolution or threshold is necessary to perform the desired test?
  • What minimum and maximum specifications does this device operate within?
  • What additional resources are required, besides testing equipment?

Step 3: View Schedule and Reserve







Check the schedule.

Look at the schedule and determine what time works best for you and your group.


The following are some suggestions you might consider when choosing your reservation time:

  • What test am I being asked to perform?
  • What configuration modes or setting does the device have internally?
  • What resolution or threshold is necessary to perform the desired test?
  • What minimum and maximum specifications does this device operate within?
  • What additional resources are required, besides testing equipment?
  • How long do I have to complete this test?
  • When will I have the time to complete this test?


Reserve.

Reserving is fairly straightforward.

  1. Clicking on bench selector will choose what bench time you will see.
  2. Clicking a date on the calendar will display the available times (blank) and the reseved times (blue text) on that day.
  3. Clicking on the time table will reserve the hour following that time.
  4. A window will appear with a summary of the time selected and a form to fill out.
  5. Submit the form and the lab manager will contact you.
  6. The lab manager will tell you if a reservation is approved or denied and why.

Keep in mind, equipment is only placed in the lab during daily business hours.
