PSPE Lab Safety Manual

Welcome to the Lab Safety Manual for the Program for Semiconductor Product Engineers!

This laboratory manual is curated by students of the the PSPE program in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering from the College of Engineering at Texas Tech University. Our mission is to provide a platform where students can actively participate in the betterment and growth of safety culture by allowing them give feedback in the form of a GIT reository hosted by GitHub Pages.
