Information for New Students

The following a quick guide for students who would like to use the PSPE Lab.

How the Lab Works


  • The PSPE lab will operate weekdays from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
  • Students will reserve days and times to use the lab.
  • Lab access will be restricted and students are expected to complete all pre-requiesites before they enter the lab.
  • Unprepared and unqualified students will not be allowed in the lab. No exceptions.

How to Use the Lab At a Glance

1. Complete Safety Requirements

  • Students will need to know the rules of the lab.
  • Students will have to complete their safety certificates and email them to the lab manager.
  • Students will be expected to maintain good houskeeping during and after using the lab.

2. Research Equipment Needed for Test

  • Students should reference the equipment list provided and decide what is necessary for their test.
  • Students should also account for resolution for certain devices and include any special requirements in their test plan.

3. Pick a Day and Time to Conduct Test

  • Students should refer to the reservation schedule to see available times.
  • Students who would like to reserve a lab bench for weekly use or a single use only should specify in their reservation submission.
  • Students are alotted a maximum of 5 hours a week.

4. Make a Reservation

  • Students will fill out the form found here.
  • The lab manager will follow up to confirm reservations.
